<h2>Why World Cup Fixtures Matter</h2>

A World Cup fixture is the official schedule that lists every match throughout the tournament. It includes the participating teams, match dates, kickoff times, venues, and tournament rounds. Fixtures begin with the group stage before progressing to the Round of 16, quarterfinals, semifinals, and the final.

Understanding the fixture helps football fans prepare ahead of time. Since most World Cup matches are played in different time zones, Filipino viewers often need to adjust their schedules to catch games live. Missing a crucial match because of incorrect timing can be frustrating, especially if you're supporting your favorite national team.

For sports bettors, fixtures also provide important information that goes beyond kickoff times. They reveal how much rest teams have between matches, potential travel schedules, and the order of opponents, all of which can influence team performance.
